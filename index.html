Let me introduce myself with a brief background on how I got here:

Hello, my name is Jawahar Kotha. I'm glad you stopped by. The vision for "Owe No One" foundation is a result of God's grace, mercy and blessings that delivered our family from decades of unnecessary trials and tribulations.

During this process I discovered ways to make it in the financial markets and be very successful. However, just having money and stuff didn't motivate me enough to go after it.

Then I discovered what was hindering me, and is plaguing so many to fail in the markets in Proverbs 21:5 ...everyone who is hasty comes only to poverty; and in Proverbs 10:4 Poor is he who works with a negligent hand, But the hand of the diligent makes rich.

And in 1 Timothy 6:10 For the love of money is a root of all kinds of evil: which some reaching after have been led astray from the faith, and have pierced themselves through with many sorrows.

I started seeking the word of God for what to do and how to use this knowledge, discoveries, revelations and talents. I started asking if and how I should or can help people that are struggling financially, which by itself perhaps, eases 90% of their burdens.

The Lord showed me in Matthew 6:33 ...seek ye first the kingdom of God, and his righteousness; and all these things shall be added unto you. Last year was the first time I studied it!

Proverbs 10:22 says The blessing of the LORD makes one rich, And He adds no sorrow with it.

And Malachi 3:10 Bring the whole tithe into the storehouse, that there may be food in my house. Test me in this", says the Lord Almighty, "and see if I will not throw open the floodgates of heaven and pour out so much blessing that there will not be room enough to store it.

Then He showed me John 15:7 If you abide in me, and my words abide in you, ask whatever you wish, and it will be done for you

As if that wasn't enough, He then showed me: Proverbs 16:3 Commit your work to the Lord, and your plans will be established.

He later took me to Psalm 37:4-5 Delight yourself also in the LORD; and he shall give you the desires of your heart. Commit your way unto the LORD; trust also in him; and he shall bring it to pass.

Those revealed to me how to help solve the problems of those struggling as in Proverbs 21:5, 10:4 and 1 Timothy 6:10. I said to myself: Wow, the WORD has all the answers and solutions to people's financial struggles to get out of debt and poverty. No wonder Jesus said to preach the gospel (the good news) to the poor - so that they can apply those solutions to themselves and be free!

Early January 2018 after much prayer, finally, I committed to obey His word, and especially Malachi 3:10. Ever since that point, it has been like a balloon drop. Don't get me wrong - that verse is not about "buying" His blessings through church. I will still have stuff to sort through, but I now have Jesus in me :), glory to God :), who is far greater and larger than any "stuff".

One of those balloons is the idea and the vision for the "Owe No One" foundation. And I think I'm ready to pop it :).

Note that I am still in the process of setting it up and establishing the necessary legal structures, etc. I will update this page when it is all done. That's my brief story.

I believe I can generate enough funds through trading the markets to support the above mission to see to it that it comes to fruition within the next 10 years - that's my goal. With partners I can do a lot more lot quicker and reach farther.
